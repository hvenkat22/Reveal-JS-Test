Financial Formulas

Net Present Value (discrete):

\(NPV = \sum_{t=1}^{T} \frac{CF_t}{(1+r)^t} - C_0\)

Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM):

\(E[R_i] = R_f + \beta_i\,(E[R_m]-R_f)\)

Leverage Ratio: \(LR = \frac{Tier\,1\,Capital}{Total\,Exposure}\)
